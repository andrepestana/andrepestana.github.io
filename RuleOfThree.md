# Rule of Three App

The Rule of Three is an app designed to help users learn and calculate proportions using the mathematical principle known as the "Rule of Three." This principle states that if you have three values, you can find a fourth value that maintains the same ratio.

## Features

- **Proportion Calculator**: Input three values to calculate the fourth value based on the Rule of Three.
- **Rule of Three Explanation**: Provides a detailed explanation of how the calculation is performed.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Simple and intuitive design for easy navigation and use.
- **Sharing Results**: Share your calculations and results via social media or messaging apps.
- **Android Platform Support**: Available on Android devices.

## Installation

To install the Rule of Three app, follow these steps:

1. Open the Google Play Store on your Android device.
2. Search for "Rule of Three App."
3. Tap on the app in the search results.
4. Click the "Install" button to download and install the app.

## Usage

1. Open the Rule of Three app on your Android device.
2. Enter the three known values in the provided input fields.
3. View the result displayed on the screen along with an explanation of the calculation.
4. Optionally, share your results using the share button.
